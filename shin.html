Shinei Nouzen

Undertaker

Shinei Nouzen, shortened to Shin, is the male protagonist of the 86 -Eighty Six- series.

About

He is the leader of the Spearhead Squadron. Among the Eighty-Six, he is known as the Reaper of the Eastern Front. His ability to hear the voices of the Legion allows him to locate them without the use of detection systems. Shin is an incredibly kind person, even after being wronged by so many people in his life. His kindness also manifests in selflessness to an extreme degree.
