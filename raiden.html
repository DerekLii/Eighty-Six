Raiden Shuga

Wehrwolf

Raiden Shuga is a primary supporting character in the 86 -Eighty Six- series.

About

He is the vice captain of the Spearhead Squadron. As a byproduct of being Shin's right hand man, he was considered the only person that was able to survive alongside the Reaper of the Eastern Front long term. Raiden is not afraid to be confrontational and upfront. Whenever he has had a problem with anyone, he would tell it to their face without fear.
