Kurena Kukumila

Gunslinger

Kurena Kukumila is a primary supporting character in the 86 -Eighty Six- series.

About

She is a platoon leader of Eighty-Sixth Strike Package's Spearhead Squadron. She serves as the squadron's long-range specialist. She harbors an intense hatred for Alba because of the murder of her parents at the hands of Republican Alba soldiers. She sharpens her shooting skills to be able to perform the same duties as Shin. However, she eventually realizes that she is not capable of being the same as him.

"Even if we die, our Reaper will take us there. I know he’ll take me there, but… But then, what about Shin? Who will carry his heart?" ― to Anju, voicing her concerns regarding Shin.
